Types of Birds

  1. Songbirds (Passerines): Examples: Sparrows, Robins, Finches, Warblers

  2. Waterfowl: Examples: Ducks, Geese, Swans

  3. Seabirds: Examples: Gulls, Puffins, Albatrosses, Penguins

  4. Pigeons and Doves: Examples: Rock Pigeon, Mourning Dove, Wood Pigeon

  5. Parrots: Examples: Macaws, Cockatoos, Budgerigars, Parakeets


Importance of Birds

  1. Pollinate plants and disperse seeds, aiding in ecosystem regeneration.

  2. Control pests naturally, reducing reliance on chemical pesticides in agriculture.

  3. Indicate environmental health, signaling pollution, habitat loss, or climate change.

  4. Contribute to culture and economy through birdwatching, art, and tourism.

  5. Enhance nutrient cycling, fertilizing soil, and promoting plant growth.


How to save birds in now-a-days.

  1. Preserve habitats by protecting forests, wetlands, and creating protected areas

  2. Limit pesticide use, opting for organic methods to protect birds.

  3. Install feeders, birdbaths, and decals to prevent window collisions.

  4. Reduce plastic use, participate in clean-ups, and prevent bird entanglement.

  5. Educate others about bird conservation and advocate for protective policies.
